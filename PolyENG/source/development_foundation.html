Development Foundation

The Foundation aims to promote the establishment of a breakthrough international science and educational center, integrating the greatest achievements of science, technology and society on the basis of the Polytechnic Museum.

The Foundation focuses on:

  • development and support of the Polytechnic Museum, one of the world's oldest science and technology museums, science and technology communicator, center for enlightenment and innovations;
  • design of the programs aimed at the development of the Polytechnic Museum, support at these programs' implementation;
  • promoting the creation of the modern interactive exhibition of international standard on the basis of the Polytechnic Museum.
